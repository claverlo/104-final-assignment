About Us

Pet Salon is a full-service grooming and care facility proudly serving dogs, cats, and other pets of all sizes and breeds. Our professional, friendly, and highly trained team provides grooming, bathing, nail clipping, ear and teeth cleaning in a clean, safe environment. We focus on hygiene, comfort, patience, and personalized attention, ensuring each pet’s unique needs are met. Our goal is to create a calm, stress-free experience that helps pets feel healthy, relaxed, confident, and genuinely happy every visit, with care, respect, trust, warmth, consistency, dedication, and love.
